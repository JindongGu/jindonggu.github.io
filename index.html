Jindong Gu

Senior Research Fellow, University of Oxford

Faculty Researcher, Google DeepMind

I am a Senior Research Fellow at University of Oxford, a member of Torr Vision Group.   I also partially work in Google DeepMind as a faculty researcher in Gemini Safety team. Prior to that, I received my Ph.D. Degree from University of Munich in 2022, advised by Prof. Volker Tresp.

My research goal is to build Responsible AI. Specifically, I am interested in the interpretability, robustness, privacy, and safety of

  • Visual Perception,
  • Foundation Model-based Understanding and Reasoning,
  • Robotic Policy and Planning,
  • and their Fusion towards General Intelligence Systems.

[Hiring!] If you like to work together with me as Ph.D/visiting student in the area of Responsible AI, please drop me an email (jindong DOT gu AT outlook DOT com), contact me on LinkedIn or Twitter I am unable to provide feedback to all queries due to a high volume of applications. You will be contacted if you are a good match.
